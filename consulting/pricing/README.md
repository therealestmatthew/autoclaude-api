# /consulting/pricing/ — STUB

Pricing models, rate cards, and the rationale behind them.

**Status:** Stub.

## When this gets filled

When you've quoted at least three engagements and want to make pricing decisions less ad-hoc. Likely artifacts:

- Day-rate / weekly-rate / project-rate ranges.
- Productized offer pricing (if `/consulting/offers/` is populated).
- Discount / upcharge logic (urgency, retainer, equity).
- The narrative justification — why this price for this value.

## What to record about each quote (for later)

Even before this gets fleshed out, when you quote an engagement, drop a short note in the engagement folder (`/consulting/engagements/<year>-<client>/pricing-rationale.md`) explaining how you priced it. That's the raw material for codifying pricing later.
