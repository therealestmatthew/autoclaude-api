# Training Deck — Anaplan End-User

Persona-shaped training material for the planners, managers, and approvers who'll use the solution post go-live.

## Sections (per persona)

- Persona context: what they're trying to accomplish, current pain points
- Workflow walk-through with screenshots
- Dashboard tour with annotated callouts
- "What changed from the old way" (critical for adoption)
- FAQ + troubleshooting
- Where to get help (Anaplan support, internal champion network)
- Quick reference / job aid (one-page PDF)

## How we use it

- Authored 4–6 weeks before go-live, finalized 2 weeks before
- Owner: Change Management Lead
- Delivered via virtual training sessions + on-demand recordings + job aids
